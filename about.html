About Us

Urban Threads is committed to curating fashion that captures today’s youth trends while remaining affordable and stylish. We strive to create an inviting in‑store and online experience where customers feel inspired and empowered by what they wear.

Strengths

  • Curated selection for university students and young professionals
  • Affordable yet stylish apparel and accessories
  • Engaging in‑store experience with personalised style consultations

Weaknesses

  • Limited brand recognition at launch
  • Inventory risk if trends shift quickly
  • Smaller marketing budget compared to established competitors

Areas for Improvement

  • Build awareness via campus events and targeted social media campaigns
  • Use agile inventory planning to adapt quickly to changing trends
  • Enhance our online presence using customer feedback for continuous improvement
